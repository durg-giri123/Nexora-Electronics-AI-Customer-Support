NEXORA TECHNICAL SUPPORT KNOWLEDGE BASE

Question:
My laptop is not turning on.

Answer:
If your Nexora laptop is not turning on:

1. Connect the original charger.
2. Ensure the charging indicator is glowing.
3. Press and hold the power button for 15 seconds.
4. Remove all USB devices.
5. Try turning it on again.

If the issue continues, visit the nearest Nexora Service Center.

Question:
My laptop battery drains quickly.

Answer:
If your laptop battery drains quickly:

1. Reduce screen brightness.
2. Enable Battery Saver mode.
3. Close unnecessary background applications.
4. Disconnect unused Bluetooth devices.
5. Update Windows and all drivers.
6. Restart the laptop.

If battery backup remains below two hours, contact Nexora Technical Support.

Keywords:
