NEXORA BILLING POLICY

GENERAL BILLING

Customers can pay using:

- UPI
- Credit Card
- Debit Card
- Net Banking

Payment confirmation is sent by email after a successful transaction.

--------------------------------------------------

PAYMENT FAILURE

If your payment fails:

- Verify that sufficient balance is available.
- Ensure your internet connection is stable.
- Retry after a few minutes.
- Contact your bank if the amount has been deducted but the order was not placed.

--------------------------------------------------

INVOICES

Invoices are generated automatically after successful payment.

Every customer receives an invoice for every purchase.

Invoices are required for:

- Warranty claims
- Returns
- Refunds
- Replacement requests

--------------------------------------------------

DOWNLOADING AN INVOICE

Customers can download their invoice from the Nexora Customer Support Portal.

Steps:

1. Log in to the Nexora Customer Support Portal.
2. Open My Orders.
3. Select your order.
4. Click Download Invoice.

If the invoice is unavailable, contact Nexora Billing Support.

--------------------------------------------------

REFUNDS

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after approval.

Refunds are credited back to the original payment method.

--------------------------------------------------

IMPORTANT NOTES

Always keep your invoice for future warranty and service requests.

Invoices cannot be modified after purchase.

Duplicate invoices can be requested from Nexora Billing Support.
