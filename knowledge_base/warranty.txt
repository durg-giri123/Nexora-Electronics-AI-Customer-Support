NEXORA WARRANTY POLICY

Warranty Duration
- All Nexora products come with a standard 1-year manufacturer warranty.
- Premium laptops include a 2-year warranty.
- Accessories carry a 6-month warranty.

Warranty Covers
- Manufacturing defects
- Hardware failure
- Motherboard defects
- Battery defects
- Display defects
- Keyboard malfunction
- Charging port failure

Warranty Does Not Cover
- Physical damage
- Water damage
- Fire damage
- Unauthorized repair
- Broken screen due to accidental drop
- Cosmetic scratches

How to Claim Warranty
1. Keep the original invoice.
2. Visit the nearest Nexora Service Center.
3. Submit the product.
4. Technician inspection.
5. Repair or replacement.

Replacement Policy
- Replacement within 7 days for manufacturing defects.
- Product must include all accessories.
- Original packaging preferred.

Documents Required
- Invoice
- Product serial number
- Valid ID proof

Extended Warranty
- Can be purchased within 30 days of purchase.
