NEXORA WARRANTY POLICY

All Nexora products come with a standard 1-year manufacturer warranty.

Products damaged due to physical misuse, water damage, or unauthorized repairs are not covered under warranty.

Customers should keep their original invoice to claim warranty services.

Warranty claims can be raised through the Nexora Customer Support Portal.

Laptop batteries and chargers are covered for one year.

Accessories such as cables and adapters are covered for six months.
