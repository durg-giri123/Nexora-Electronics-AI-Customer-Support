NEXORA WARRANTY POLICY

GENERAL WARRANTY

- All Nexora laptops come with a standard 1-year manufacturer warranty.
- Laptop batteries are covered for one year.
- Laptop chargers are covered for one year.
- Accessories such as USB cables, HDMI cables, adapters, and power cords are covered for six months.

WHAT IS COVERED

The warranty covers manufacturing defects including:

- Hardware malfunction
- Motherboard failure
- Display defects
- Keyboard defects
- Touchpad malfunction
- Factory battery defects
- Factory charger defects
- Internal hardware failure

WHAT IS NOT COVERED

Warranty does not cover:

- Physical damage
- Water or liquid damage
- Fire damage
- Accidental drops
- Broken screens caused by impact
- Unauthorized repair attempts
- Damage caused by third-party accessories
- Cosmetic scratches
- Theft or loss

WARRANTY CLAIM PROCESS

Customers must:

1. Visit the Nexora Customer Support Portal.
2. Submit the product serial number.
3. Upload the purchase invoice.
4. Describe the issue.
5. Wait for verification by the support team.

After approval:

- Pickup may be scheduled.
- Customers may visit an authorized service center.
- Repair or replacement will be decided after inspection.

REPLACEMENT POLICY

Replacement is available only if:

- Product was damaged during delivery.
- Replacement request is raised within 7 days.
- Manufacturing defect is confirmed by technicians.

Warranty does not guarantee replacement for every issue.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS

Customers should keep:

- Original invoice
- Product serial number
- Warranty registration details
- Purchase confirmation email (optional)

SERVICE CENTERS

Warranty service is available only through authorized Nexora service centers.

Using unauthorized repair shops immediately voids the warranty.

IMPORTANT NOTES

Warranty begins from the invoice date.

Warranty cannot be transferred to another customer.

Replacement products continue under the original warranty period.

Extended warranty plans are currently not available.
